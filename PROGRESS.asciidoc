Klam Implementation Progress
============================

This document tracks the progress of Klam towards a feature-complete 1.0.0
release.

Implemented
-----------

Language Features
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
* Reader
* Environment
** Global assignment namespace
** Global function namespace
* Code Generation
** Atoms
** Abstractions
** Functions
** Special forms (see marked primimitives below)
** Application
*** Functions and abstractions:
**** Full application
**** Partial application
**** Currying
** Self tail-call optimization

Primitives
~~~~~~~~~~
* Boolean Operations
** +if+ (special form)
** +and+ (special form)
** +or+ (special form)
** +cond+ (special form)
* Assignments
** +set+
** +value+
* Lists
** +cons+
** +hd+
** +tl+
** +cons?+
* Error Handling
** +simple-error+
** +trap-error+ (special form)
** +error-to-string+
* Generic Functions
** +defun+ (special form)
** +lambda+ (special form)
** +let+ (special form)
** +eval-kl+
** +freeze+ (special form)
** +type+ (special form)
* Arithmetic
** +++
** +-+
** +*+
** +/+
** +>+
** +<+
** +>=+
** +\<=+
** +number?+

Ruby Interoperation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
* Ruby \<\-> Kl converters
** Array \<\-> List

Not Yet Implemented
