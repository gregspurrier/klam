Klam Implementation Progress
============================

This document tracks the progress of Klam towards a feature-complete 1.0.0
release.

Implemented
-----------

Language Features
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
* Reader
* Environment
* Code Generation
** Atoms
*** Booleans
*** Numbers
*** Strings
*** Symbols
*** Functions from global environment
**** Currently only when applied to all arguments

Primitives
~~~~~~~~~~
* Boolean Operations
** +if+ (special form)
** +and+ (special form)
** +or+ (special form)
* Assignments
** +set+
** +value+
* Lists
** +cons+
** +hd+
** +tl+
** +cons?+
* Generic Functions
** +defun+ (special form)
** +eval-kl+
* Arithmetic
** +++
** +-+
** +*+
** +/+
** +>+
** +<+
** +>=+
** +\<=+
** +number?+


Ruby Interoperation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
* Ruby \<\-> Kl converters
** Array \<\-> List


Not Yet Implemented
-------------------

Language Features
